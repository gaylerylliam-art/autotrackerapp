AI & OCR Transparency. Receipts you upload may be processed using AI OCR to extract details such as supplier name, amount, and date. This data is used only to pre-fill your expense form and improve your experience. Your receipt data is never shared or sold.
📋1. Information We Collect
We collect only what's necessary to provide AutoTrack's features:
  • Account data — name, email address, password (hashed), and profile preferences
  • Vehicle data — make, model, year, plate number, odometer readings, fuel type
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, vendor names, and notes you enter
  • Uploaded content — receipt images and PDFs processed via AI OCR for field extraction
  • Usage data — app interactions, feature usage, crash reports, and session analytics
  • Device data — device type, OS version, and language settings
⚙️2. How We Use Your Data
  • Provide expense tracking, reporting, and maintenance reminders
  • Process receipt images using AI OCR to auto-fill expense fields
  • Generate insights and cost analytics for your vehicles
  • Send push notifications and service reminders you've opted into
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
🛡️
We respect your privacy and never sell your data to advertisers, data brokers, or any third party. Full stop.
🤖3. AI & OCR Processing
AutoTrack uses AI-powered Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to extract information from receipts you upload. Here's exactly what that means for you:
  • Receipts are sent to a secure AI processing service to extract text
  • Extracted fields (supplier, date, amount, VAT) are returned to your device and pre-filled in the form
  • You can review, correct, or override any AI-extracted field before saving
  • Receipt images are not permanently stored beyond your document vault
  • AI data is used only to improve your experience, not for advertising
"AI helps automate your entries, but you stay in control."
🔒4. Data Storage & Security
  • All data is stored on encrypted cloud servers (AES-256)
  • Data in transit is protected using TLS 1.3
  • Authentication uses industry-standard secure token mechanisms
  • We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorised AutoTrack systems only
  • Backups are encrypted and stored in geographically redundant locations
⚖️5. Your Rights (GDPR & Global)
Regardless of where you are in the world, you have the following rights:
  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update or correct inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion — Request full deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — Export your expense data in CSV or PDF format
  • Objection — Opt out of non-essential data processing at any time
  • Restriction — Limit how your data is processed while a dispute is resolved
To exercise any right, contact us at privacy@autotrack.app or use the "Delete My Data" option in Account settings.
"You can request deletion of your data anytime."
🔗6. Third-Party Services
AutoTrack uses trusted third-party services to operate. Each is bound by strict data processing agreements:
  • Cloud Infrastructure — AWS / Google Cloud (encrypted storage)
  • Authentication — Secure identity provider with MFA support
  • AI / OCR — Receipt processing (data not retained post-extraction)
  • Analytics — Anonymised usage analytics (no personal identifiers shared)
  • Push Notifications — Firebase Cloud Messaging (device token only)
🌍7. International Use
AutoTrack is available globally. If you use the app outside your home country, your data may be processed in a different region. We ensure all cross-border transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required.
The app is operated in compliance with UAE data protection regulations and applicable international standards.
